ABOUT US

Discover a haven of tranquility just a stone's throw away from the bustling Bangalore-Mangalore highway. Nestled in the idyllic countryside, this elegant Farm Retreat offers the perfect escape from the city's chaos. Located a mere 2-hour drive (120km) from Bangalore city, it promises a rejuvenating experience that will leave you enchanted. Picture yourself in this serene setting, where nature's beauty unfolds before your eyes. Follow us on Instagram at "mnd_farm_retreat" to catch a glimpse of the breathtaking landscapes that await you. Designed to accommodate a group of 12-16 individuals, our Farm Retreat offers a range of amenities to ensure your comfort and enjoyment. Step into the fully furnished living/lounge area, complete with a 65" TV, providing the ideal space to unwind and relax. What sets this retreat apart is the indoor private pool, accessible from all rooms. Imagine waking up to a refreshing swim, or enjoying a leisurely dip under the starry sky. With three private rooms, each boasting attached washrooms, this haven is perfect for families and couples seeking a touch of modern-classic elegance. For the adventurous souls and backpacking troupes, we have a fourth room or dormitory, equipped with six single sleepers and attached washrooms. Gather around the center table and share stories that will create memories to last a lifetime.

As you explore the retreat's surroundings, you'll find a charming sit-out area, complete with swing seats and comfortable chairs. Embrace the gentle breeze as you unwind in this peaceful space. Encircling the sit-out area is a well-maintained garden/lawn, meticulously fenced to provide privacy and security. At our Farm Retreat, immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, indulge in the comforts of modern amenities, and create unforgettable memories. Whether you seek a romantic getaway, a fun-filled family adventure, or a bonding experience with friends, this is the place where dreams come true. Escape the ordinary and embark on an extraordinary journey at our Farm Retreat. Book your stay today and reconnect with the essence of nature.
